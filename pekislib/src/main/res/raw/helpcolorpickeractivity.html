Colors

Here you can edit the colors displayed in the color wheel.
Current item name is displayed and a small red pin points to its color.
Play with the three sliders (R/G/B (or H/S/V)) to change its color or key in a RGB value.
Press on current item name (or spin the wheel) to select next item.
Press on current color space to select next color space (RGB or HSV).
